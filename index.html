About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name's Neeni, and I graduated from Elon University in May 2017 with a B.A. in Psychology. Shortly afterwards, I moved to Washington, D.C. to work for Greenpeace. I first worked in Human Resources, then moved to I.T., which sparked my interest in learning how to code.

Outside of work, I like to draw, paint, cook, and play with dogs. I also like to travel, which I did during a lot of during the spring semester of my junior year of college. I spent 3.5 months in Florence, where I became a pasta aficionado and explored other countries, such as Switzerland, the U.K., and the Czech Republic.
